Terms & Conditions

1. Browsing and Accounts
Users may browse listings without an account. An account is required to message, make offers, buy items, list products, open disputes, and manage payments.

2. Offers and Payment Authorization
Buyers may be required to add a valid payment method before submitting an offer. If a seller accepts the offer, payment may be authorized or held immediately.

3. Seller Shipping Obligation
Once an order is confirmed, the seller must ship the item within 5 business days. Failure to ship may result in cancellation, penalties, or account action.

4. Delivery and Buyer Review
After delivery, the buyer has 48 hours to approve the transaction or open a dispute for damage, fraud, incorrect item, or major misrepresentation.

5. Automatic Release of Funds
If no dispute is opened within the 48-hour window, the transaction is considered accepted and funds are released automatically to the seller, less platform fees.

6. Disputes
SportsSwap may review evidence, messages, photos, shipping details, and listing descriptions to decide the outcome of a dispute.

7. Platform Fees
SportsSwap may deduct transaction fees, processing fees, shipping labels, or dispute-related adjustments where applicable.

8. Prohibited Conduct
Users may not sell counterfeit goods, stolen items, prohibited equipment, or intentionally misleading listings.

9. Demo Disclaimer
This version is a front-end demo only and does not process live payments or legal agreements.
